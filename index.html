About Me

My name is Isaac and I am a Marine Corps veteran, software engineer and firearms instructor. During my military service, I was a Field Artillery Cannoneer and have been deployed to Japan and Korea for training and readiness. After my service I went into manufacturing for about six months before I decided to obtain my commercial drivers license and switch careers to truck driving. I spent the next five years driving trucks and after that I decided it was time for a change. I chose to become a software engineer.

Currently, I build websites and apps for small business and non-profits. Part time, I am a firearms instructor. I educate people on the basics of firearms handling, safety and marksmanship.

I specialize in Python, JavaScript, HTML, CSS and some C++. I also have experience working with Django and Django REST backend frameworks and also have experience with Vue.js and Bootstrap frontend frameworks
